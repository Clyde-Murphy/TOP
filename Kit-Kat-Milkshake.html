Kit Kat Milkshake

All you need is a Kit Kat bar, ice cream, milk, vanilla extract, chocolate syrup, whipped cream, and a blender and you can make your own in just a few minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 Kit Kat bar (broken)
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream (or frozen yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Hershey's chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Combine ice cream, Kit Kat bar, milk, and vanilla extract in blender. Squeeze chocolate syrup on top.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into your favorite glass and top with whipped cream and chocolate syrup. Enjoy!
